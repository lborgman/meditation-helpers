  • ChatGPT
    Very instructive. I will build the instructions into this app. "Pranayama is a yogic breathing practice that focuses on controlling the breath to influence your body and mind."
  • Claude AI
    It shows a little app in the answer. "Here's an interactive pranayama guide with five core techniques."
  • deepseek
    Short info plus some instructions. "Pranayama is a core component of yoga, often referred to as the "fourth limb" in Patanjali's Yoga Sutras."
  • Gemini
    Short info plus some instructions. "Pranayama is the ancient practice of breath control, originating from yoga traditions."
  • Le Chat (Mistral)
    Short info plus some instructions. "Pranayama is the ancient yogic practice of breath control. It’s a central part of yoga and is used to improve physical health, mental clarity, and emotional balance."
